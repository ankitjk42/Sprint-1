ABOUT US

1. Titan - Titan Neo IV watch.

Titan is one of the best brands of watches in India. It is a venture from Tata, so the products are undoubtedly the best in the market. It has many collections, from regular watches for men and women to the women-specific Raga collection. The products are known for their longevity, unique designs, and excellent style. In other words, Titan has watches to cater to the needs of different customers. According to several studies, it owns about 60% of the watch market, paving the way for a paradigm shift. It also features an elegant and luxurious watch collection known as Nebula, where the watches are made from pure 18K gold and encrusted with precious stones.



2. Fastrack - Fastrack analog black watch

Fastrack was born in 1998 as a subsidiary of the Tata brand. It is one of the most famous watch brands on the market and is known for its high-quality watches, unique fashion statements, and affordable prices. The beautifully designed brand logo on the dial makes Fastrack watches the best. You will get regular watches for casual wear and precious ones that can be reserved for special occasions. The best part is the watches are pretty colourful, from the band to the dial. You will find beautiful combinations like a white band with a light sky blue dial, a black band with a gradient pink dial, and so on.



3. Sonata - Sonata Analog Blue Dial Men's Watch

The next brand that you should look into is Sonata. It is also considered one of India's oldest watch brands and is still quite popular and dominant in the market. Gold-plated watches are the statement of this brand, as most of them are suitable for men or women over 30 years of age. But to meet the competition in the market, Sonata has recently launched several collections for young stars, casual wear, and regular office wear.



4. Timex - Timex Analog Blue Dial Watch

Timex is the one to go with if you want stylish and affordable watches. Although the brand is available mainly online, its watches are outstanding and will help take your fashion level to the next level. What makes the brand more commendable is the variety of watches with an excellent, style statement that would have cost you over a thousand bucks for some other brands.
